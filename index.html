Privacy Policy — The Simple Budget App

Last updated: November 6, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to The Simple Budget App ("we", "our", or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your information. This Privacy Policy explains how our app handles data.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data. All information you enter into the app (for example budgets, expenses, categories, notes) is stored only on your device.

3. Data Storage

All data created or managed within The Simple Budget App is stored locally on your phone's internal storage. We do not transmit any information to external servers, cloud services, or third parties.

If you uninstall the app or clear its app data, any information saved by the app will be permanently deleted from your device.

4. Permissions

The app may request minimal permissions required for proper functionality (for example, permission to read/write local storage). The app does not request access to contacts, camera, microphone, precise location, or other personal data unless explicitly stated in a future update.

5. Third-Party Services

The Simple Budget App does not use any tracking or analytics services (for example Google Analytics, Firebase) and does not include advertising networks, external databases, or remote APIs that collect user data.

6. Children’s Privacy

The Simple Budget App is suitable for general audiences. Because we do not collect any personal information, we do not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13.

7. Your Control

Since your data is stored only on your device, you have full control over it. You can delete or edit data using the app's built-in features or by uninstalling the app to remove its local data.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of the page. Any material changes will be described in the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@simplesbudget.example.

Thank you for using The Simple Budget App. Your privacy matters to us.
